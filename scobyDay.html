What to do on scoby day

Back to the homepage

On scoby day you need to do the following:
1. Make a scuby tea (recipe shown below)
2. Transfer liquid from 1st to 2nd bottle using the funnel, keeping the scoby in the 1st
3. Take 3rd bottle out of fridge
4. Transfer liquid from 2nd bottle to 3rd bottle usin the funnel
5. Replace scoby in the fridge
6. Add scoby tea to 1st bottle with the scoby in it
7. Make sure to drink all of the scoby drink over the next week

Warning: you must drain the scoby drink throuroughly before drinking it

How to make the scoby tea:
1. Boil 1 litre of water
2. Add 4 bags of green tea and leave to brew
3. After it has finished brewing, add 80g of sugar
